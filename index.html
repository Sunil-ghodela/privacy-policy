Privacy Policy — Alankar Companion

Last updated: May 2026

Alankar Companion ("the app") is a Hindustani classical music practice companion published by the developer of the Riyaaz project.

What we collect

We do not operate our own servers or user accounts. Practice data (sessions, history) stays on your device in a local database.

Data stored on your device

  • Saved practice sessions and history you generate in the app
  • Optional tanpura audio files you choose to import from your device storage
  • App preferences (theme mood, sargam script, subscription flag)

Third-party services

Google AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and usage data to show ads, subject to Google's Privacy Policy. You can limit ad personalization in your Android settings.

The app requests INTERNET permission for ads. Audio playback and practice content work offline except for ad loading.

Permissions

Read media/audio — only to let you pick tanpura MP3 files you already have on your device. We do not upload these files anywhere.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
